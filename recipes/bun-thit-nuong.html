Bun Thit Nuong

Ingedients:

  • 500g vermicelli
  • 500g porkbelly
  • 15g shallots
  • 30g garlic
  • 1 tbsp fish sauce
  • 2 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1 tbsp siracha
  • 2 tsp chilli oil (optional)
  • crushed peanuts (optional)
  • thai chilli (optional)
  • beansprouts (optional)
  • freshly ground pepper
  • greens
  • nuoc mam

    • Directions:

      Marinade:

    1. Mince the garlic and shallots.
    2. Combine the minced garlic and shllots with fish sauce, sesmae oil, chilli oil, and a pinch on gound pepper.

      Dish:

    1. Thinly slice the porkbelly and mix will with the marinade. Set aside for at least 2 hours, or ideally overnight in the fridge.
    2. Preheat airfrier to 350 F, cook pork for 25 min stiriing occasionally.
    3. While the pork is in the airfrier, slice greens thinning and prep vermicelli.
    4. When the pork is done, mix equal parts honey and siracha to make a glaze. Mix well with the pork and cook for an additonal 2 min.
    5. To plate, start with a handfull of vermicelli then add the pork on top. Optionally garish with greens, bean sprouts, and crushed peanuts, and a drizzle of nuoc mam.
